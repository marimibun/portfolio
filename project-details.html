Case Study

Lifeplanner

A comprehensive case study on improving the user flow and visual design of a financial management app.

Overview

This project was a deep dive into the user experience of a popular financial app. My goal was to identify key pain points through user research and redesign critical features to be more intuitive, efficient, and visually appealing.

Problem Statement

Users were struggling with the app's complex navigation and cluttered interface, leading to low engagement and high drop-off rates on key financial tasks.

My Role

Lead UX Designer, responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity mockups.

The Design Process

My process followed a user-centric approach, beginning with in-depth research to truly understand user needs.

  • **Discovery & Research:** Conducted user interviews and surveys to gather qualitative data.
  • **Information Architecture:** Restructured the app's navigation to be more logical and intuitive.
  • **Wireframing & Prototyping:** Created low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes for usability testing.
  • **Visual Design:** Developed a new, clean visual system with a focus on accessibility and clarity.
